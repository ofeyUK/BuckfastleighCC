    Welcome

    Welcome to the online home of Buckfastleigh Cricket Club. We play a range of Sunday friendlies, mid-week 20-20 matches, and as of 2023 have a team in the Devon Cricket League.

    We are a team made up of a wide variety of skills from experienced older players, keen youngsters, those who have played many years and those just beginning to learn the sport. We also welcome non playing members who would like to get involved with any aspect of the club. This could be helping with the maintenance of the ground, helping with the teas, umpiring, or scoring.

    If you are interested in getting involved, please get in touch.
